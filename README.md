# Wang-Sha-Exhibitions

**A Cultural Icon: Wang Sha’s Life and Career**

**3 September 2025**

Wang Sha, born **Heng Kim Ching** (王锦清), was a legendary figure in Singaporean and Malaysian entertainment. Renowned for his exceptional comedic talent and his heartfelt portrayal of everyday life, he brought warmth and humour to audiences across generations.

Together with his longtime partner **Ye Feng** (野峰, 1932–1995), he formed the iconic comic duo affectionately known as **Ah Pui (Fat)** and **Ah San (Skinny)**. Their contrasting physiques and natural chemistry made them one of the most beloved comedy pairs in the region, leaving an indelible mark on local culture.

Beyond the stage, Wang Sha was a dedicated mentor and cultural advocate, remembered for his professionalism, warmth, and deep care for society.
